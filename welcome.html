WELCOME EXPERIENCE CONTACT

Christie Corcoran

Welcome to my site! I have been in the software development world for over 28 years. Working with others to build amazing products is my passion. Every player of the product lifecycle is critical to its success. I find joy in seeing everyone come to the table. My passion for collaborating with others has sustained me in this field that can at times be grueling with pressure and deadlines. In the end, the taste of sweet success with a team that is a second family is worth all the effort.

I stumbled into programming while in college at North Carolina State University (Go Wolfpack!). Not knowing what to major in, Computer Science felt different from the other Engineering curriculums I was considering. My first class was love at first sight. I was fortunate to find a co-op at IBM and had several fabulous development rotations there getting to work on products such as ISPF, CSP, and even producing and supporting my own internal IBM tool.

Upon graduation, I landed a job at SAS, a company well renowned for their data analytics and amazing work-life balance. The next 20 years flew by as I worked on the Base SAS platform as well as exciting products such as SAS Enterprise Guide and SAS Studio. There were also a few failed projects along the way but that is all part of the R in R&D. Eventually though, I was itching for change. I had been asked on several occasions to consider management and this time, I agreed. There, I discovered and developed skills I never thought possible. The thrill of having a team, discovering their strengths and weaknesses, and then capitalizing on those strengths to deliver high-quality software invigorated me.
